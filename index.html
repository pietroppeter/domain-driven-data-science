What is Logistics?

"an army without its baggage train is lost; without provisions it is lost; without bases of supply it is lost" Art of War, Sun Tzu, 5th BC
  • def: Logistics is the process of strategically managing the procurement, movement and storage of materials, parts and finished inventory

(and related information flows)

  • def continues: "...through the organisation and its marketing channels in such a way that current and future profitability are maximised through the cost-effective fulfillment of orders"
  • logistics is homonym of the logistics curve, comes from French Marechal de Logis, the officer in charge of the functioning of an army
  • logistics is now a cornerstone of international trade
